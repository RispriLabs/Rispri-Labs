About Us

Founded in 1999 in Agra, the eco-city of India, Ram Chemical Enterprises has been a pioneer in providing innovative water treatment solutions. As an ISO 9001:2015 certified organization, we specialize in manufacturing and supplying a comprehensive range of water treatment chemicals, equipment, and plants. From Reverse Osmosis Systems and Water Softening Plants to Effluent Treatment Chemicals and Descaling Compounds, our solutions are designed to meet the diverse needs of industries across the nation.

At Ram Chemical Enterprises, we believe in building trust through transparency, reliability, and customer-centricity. Our dedicated team of engineers, technicians, and quality analysts ensures every solution we offer adheres to the highest standards of safety and efficiency. Driven by a vision to create awareness about water conservation and provide comprehensive treatment solutions under one roof, we are not just a vendor but a trusted partner in addressing water-related challenges. Let us help you secure a sustainable future, one drop at a time.
