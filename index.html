About Us

At Rispri Labs, we are committed to building a trusted brand that serves as a cornerstone for sustainable and environment-friendly solutions. With a vision to empower industries and organizations, we operate through two distinct verticals:

  • Rispri Laboratory: Specializing in precise testing and analysis services for water, food, soil, and industrial materials.
  • Ram Chemical Enterprises: Delivering innovative water treatment solutions and sustainable systems to address environmental challenges.

At Rispri Labs, every solution we offer is a step towards a greener, cleaner, and more sustainable future. Together, we aim to create a world where environmental responsibility and industrial growth go hand in hand.
